Funding Guidelines

A well-planned funding strategy increases the chances of reaching your campaign goal and earning backer trust.

  • 💰 Set a realistic funding goal.
  • 📅 Choose an appropriate campaign duration.
  • 🎁 Offer meaningful rewards.
  • 📊 Clearly explain fund allocation.
  • 🤝 Keep supporters informed.

"Transparency builds trust. The clearer your funding plan, the more confident backers will feel supporting your project."
